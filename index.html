About Me

I’m an undergraduate astrophysics researcher at Georgia State University, studying barium dwarfs and white dwarf companions using Gaia data, high-res spectroscopy, and interferometry. I work with Dr. Russel White and CHARA data to understand binary evolution and s-process enrichment.
